About Brian

Brian is a software engineer with energy, ambition, and chill vibes. Having recently graduated from Flatiron School with a certification in Software Engineering, he loves creating projects he himself finds useful.

With a background in healthcare, he is experienced with solving complex problems and is always looking to learn from those around him.

When not programming he can be found cycling, gaming, or preventing natural selection from claiming his Chihuahua.
