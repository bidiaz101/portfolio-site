Get to know Brian!

Who is Brian?

Brian is a software engineer with energy, ambition, and chill vibes. Having recently graduated from Flatiron School with a certification in Software Engineering, he loves creating projects he himself finds useful. He is inspired by a anecdote from a stand-up comedy special by a self-proclaimed Renaissance man.

I just put out that E.P. ... I was mixing and I was like "I wanna see how it sounds in the car" and I picked up a friend of mine. She got in. She was like "Is this you?" and I was like "Yeah üòè" and she goes "You listen to your own music??" - Donald Glover, Weirdo

While not (yet) a Renaissance man, Brian find himself in a field where you not only aren't judged for enjoying your own creations, but encouraged! When creating a new web app, he asks himself, "Would I use this?"

Where did he come from?

I was encouraged to try programming by a fellow SMU alum, and caught the coding bug (pun intended). I recognized it as an intersection between abstract logic and real-world application. After discovering the rabbit-hole of languages and frameworks, I'm excited to jump right in!

Upon graduation from Southern Methodist University he started a career in healthcare where he used drive and collaborative skills to have positive experiences with other technicians. My attention to detail and commitment has helped patients and doctors alike.
